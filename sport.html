Achievements on international level (representing South Africa):

~ 2015 7th KWF World Cup in Chitose, Japan – won gold medal for mixed unison kata.
~ 2017 8th KWF World Cup in Malta – won bronze medal for mixed unison kata. There is a mention of my achievement in this article: https://sandtonchronicle.co.za/193989/parktown-dojo-shakes-world-order/

Achievements on national level:

~ 2017 BKI-KWF South Africa National Championships (10/06/2017) – won three golds. Gold for MALE OPEN KUMITE (born in 2003), gold for MALE OPEN KATA (born in 2003) and gold for MIXED JIYU IPPON KUMITE (born in 2003 - 2004). Grand champion for 2017.
~ 2019 BKI-KWF South Africa National Championships (31/08/2019) – won gold for MALE YOUTH BLACK & BROWN BELT KATA (born in 1999 to 2003) and bronze for MALE BLACK BELT KATA (born in 2003 and older).
~ Achieved gold in kata for each regional and national championship for 7 years consecutively within my age group.

* Note: This is not an exhaustive list but are the most notable.
